About Welcom to Travel Melb Mobile App

This app was designed by Deakin University Students for SIT 305 Advanced Apps Development

Authors: Chris Hurley , Daniel Ruffolo

How It works

The App Adreses the complexity of navagating around Melbournes elaborate transport network through utilising various API's and joining the dots in a manner than minimises the ammount of steps requred to figure out what the best way is to get to where you need to be'


The Nitty Gritty Details

Our app is based on the Ionic Framework. We utilise open source cordova libraries to provide a native app experience

https://ionicframework.com/docs/


Ionic is based on google's Angular JS framework. this gives us a common ground to program with

https://docs.angularjs.org/api


Our app Ties in with PTV's unnoficial Swagger API to extract timetable and geopositions of nearby transit stops'

https://timetableapi.ptv.vic.gov.au/swagger/ui/index


Our app is cental to a google maps instance and relies heavily upon the maps API . The Maps API is essential to simplifiying the process of traveling on public transport.

https://developers.google.com/maps/
